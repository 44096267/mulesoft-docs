= To Add Terms and Conditions in Exchange

. Log into your private Exchange.
. Click an asset.
. Click Edit.
. Click Add Terms and Conditions: image:ex2-terms.png[ex2-terms]
. Use the editor to specify the terms and conditions for this asset.
. Click Save As Draft.
. Publish.
